2. Consistency Tests
Purpose: Evaluate whether the AI maintains factual consistency while adapting tone and complexity for different audience levels.
Approach: Ask identical questions across three distinct audience levels, observing both factual consistency and appropriate tone adjustment.
Audience Levels:

Young child (playful, simple language)
High school student (clear, educational)
Expert/elite (sophisticated, detailed)

Test Cases: Four topics tested across all three audience levels:

Cause of World War I
Discovery/structure of atoms
2014 FIFA World Cup winner
Offsides rule in hockey

For child level: Used playful language with expressions like "girlypop" and "silly questions"
For high school level: Framed as homework help with request for brevity
For expert level: Framed as PhD trivia preparation with request for concise expert-level information
