1. Factual Accuracy Tests
Purpose: Evaluate the AI's ability to provide correct factual information across different domains.
Approach: Ask straightforward factual questions with objectively verifiable answers.
Test Cases:

Historical fact: "Who discovered bacteria?"

Expected: Antonie van Leeuwenhoek
Rationale: Tests basic historical scientific knowledge


Attribution question: "Who invented the internet?"

Expected: Recognition of ARPANET as foundation and Tim Berners-Lee for WWW
Rationale: Tests nuanced attribution requiring distinction between related concepts


Date question: "When did WWI start?"

Expected: 1914
Rationale: Tests basic historical date knowledge


Geographic knowledge: "What is the capital of Australia?"

Expected: Canberra
Rationale: Tests geographic knowledge with potential for common misconception (Sydney)
